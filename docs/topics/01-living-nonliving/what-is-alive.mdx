# What Makes Something Alive?

Look around you! Can you see plants, animals, and people? These are all **living things**! But what makes something alive?

## The 5 Signs of Life

Living things can do 5 special things:

### 1. They Grow ğŸŒ±

Living things start small and get bigger!
- A baby grows into an adult
- A tiny seed grows into a big tree
- A puppy grows into a big dog

### 2. They Need Food and Water ğŸğŸ’§

All living things need to eat and drink to stay alive!
- You eat food for energy
- Plants drink water from the soil
- Animals hunt or find food

### 3. They Breathe ğŸ’¨

Living things need air to stay alive!
- You breathe air through your nose
- Fish breathe air from water through gills
- Plants take in air through tiny holes in their leaves

### 4. They Move ğŸƒ

Living things can move from place to place!
- You walk, run, and jump
- Birds fly in the sky
- Fish swim in the water
- Even plants move slowly toward sunlight!

### 5. They Have Babies ğŸ‘¶

Living things can make more living things like themselves!
- People have babies
- Dogs have puppies
- Birds lay eggs that hatch into baby birds
- Plants make seeds that grow into new plants

## Remember This!

:::tip Key Idea
If something can **grow, eat, breathe, move, and have babies**, it's a **living thing**!
:::
