# Living and Non-living Things

Welcome to your first science topic! Let's learn about the amazing world of living and non-living things!

## What Will You Learn?

In this topic, you will discover:

- What makes something alive
- How to tell if something is living or non-living
- The special things living things can do
- Examples of living and non-living things around you

## Lessons

1. **What Makes Something Alive?** - Learn the special things living things can do
2. **Non-living Things Around Us** - Discover things that are not alive
3. **Sorting Living and Non-living** - Practice telling them apart!

## Why Is This Important?

Understanding living and non-living things helps us:
- Take care of plants and animals
- Know what needs food and water
- Understand our world better
