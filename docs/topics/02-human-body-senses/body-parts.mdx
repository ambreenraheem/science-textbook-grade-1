# Parts of Your Body

Your body has many different parts, and each part does an important job! Let's explore!

Your head has many important parts:

### Brain
- Inside your head
- Helps you think, learn, and remember
- Controls your whole body
- Like a computer inside you!

### Face
- **Eyes** - help you see
- **Nose** - helps you smell and breathe
- **Ears** - help you hear
- **Mouth** - helps you eat, talk, and taste

### Hair
- Grows on your head
- Helps keep your head warm
- Comes in different colors!

### Arms and Hands
- **Arms** - help you reach and carry things
- **Elbows** - help your arms bend
- **Hands** - help you hold and touch things
- **Fingers** - 5 on each hand, help you pick up small things

### Legs and Feet
- **Legs** - help you walk, run, and jump
- **Knees** - help your legs bend
- **Feet** - help you stand and balance
- **Toes** - 5 on each foot, help you balance

### Chest
- Your **heart** is inside - it pumps blood through your body
- Your **lungs** are inside - they help you breathe

### Tummy (Belly)
- Your **stomach** is inside - it helps digest food
- Gives you energy to play and learn!
