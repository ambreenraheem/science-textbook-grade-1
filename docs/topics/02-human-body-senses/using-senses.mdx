# Using Your Senses

Now let's practice using your 5 senses to explore the world around you!

## Exploring with Sight üëÄ

**Look around you right now!**

What can you see?
- What **colors** do you see? (red, blue, green, yellow?)
- What **shapes** can you find? (circles, squares, triangles?)
- Can you see something **far away**? Something **close**?

:::tip Try This!
Close your eyes for 10 seconds, then open them. What's the first thing you see?
:::

## Exploring with Hearing üëÇ

**Listen carefully!**

What can you hear?
- Are there **voices** talking?
- Can you hear any **music**?
- Do you hear **quiet sounds** like a clock ticking?
- Do you hear **loud sounds** like a car outside?

:::tip Try This!
Be very quiet for one minute. How many different sounds can you hear?
:::

## Exploring with Smell üëÉ

**Take a deep breath through your nose!**

What can you smell?
- Does it smell **good** (like flowers or food)?
- Does it smell **bad** (like trash)?
- Can you smell **nothing at all**?

:::tip Fun Activity!
At dinner time, smell your food before you eat it. Can you guess what it is just by smelling?
:::

## Exploring with Taste üëÖ

**Think about your last meal!**

What did you taste?
- Was it **sweet** like fruit or candy?
- Was it **salty** like chips?
- Was it **sour** like lemon?
- Did you like the taste?

:::tip Remember!
Never taste something unless a grown-up says it's okay! Some things are not safe to eat!
:::

## Exploring with Touch ü§ö

**Feel different things around you!**

Touch something and describe it:
- Is it **soft** or **hard**?
- Is it **smooth** or **rough**?
- Is it **warm** or **cold**?
- Is it **wet** or **dry**?

:::tip Try This!
Close your eyes and touch different objects. Can you guess what they are just by feeling them?
:::

## Using More Than One Sense!

Usually, you use more than one sense at the same time!

### Eating an Apple üçé
- **See** - It's red and round
- **Smell** - It smells sweet and fresh
- **Touch** - It feels smooth and cool
- **Hear** - It makes a "crunch" sound when you bite it
- **Taste** - It tastes sweet and juicy

### Playing Outside üå≥
- **See** - Green grass and blue sky
- **Hear** - Birds singing and wind blowing
- **Smell** - Fresh air and flowers
- **Touch** - Soft grass under your feet
- **Taste** - Maybe you have a snack outside!

## Senses Help You Learn

Your senses help you learn new things every day!

At school:
- You **see** the board and books
- You **hear** your teacher talking
- You **touch** your pencil to write
- You might **smell** crayons or markers
- You **taste** your lunch

## Senses Keep You Safe!

Your senses warn you about danger:

- **Sight** - See a car coming, so you don't cross the street
- **Hearing** - Hear a fire alarm, so you know to leave
- **Smell** - Smell smoke, so you know there might be fire
- **Touch** - Feel something too hot, so you pull your hand away
- **Taste** - Taste something bad, so you don't swallow it
