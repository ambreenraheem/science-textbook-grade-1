# The Five Senses

You have **5 special senses** that help you explore the world! Let's learn about each one!

- Shapes (circles, squares, triangles)
- Your family and friends
- Books, toys, and nature

**What can you hear?**
- Your teacher talking
- Music and songs
- Birds chirping
- Cars honking
- Thunder during a storm

:::tip Fun Fact

:::

Your **nose** helps you smell!

**What can you smell?**
- Flowers in the garden
- Food cooking in the kitchen
- Rain on the ground
- Your favorite perfume or soap

:::tip Safety Tip
Your nose can warn you! If you smell smoke, it means there might be fire nearby.
:::

## 5. Touch - Feeling with Your Skin ğŸ¤š

Your **skin** all over your body helps you feel things!

**What can you feel?**
- Soft (like a pillow or pet fur)
- Hard (like a table or wall)
- Hot (like warm water)
- Cold (like ice cream)
- Rough (like tree bark)
- Smooth (like glass)

:::tip Safety
Your sense of touch warns you! If something is too hot or too cold, your skin tells you to move away!
:::

## Why Are Senses Important?

Your 5 senses help you:
- Learn about the world
- Stay safe from danger
- Enjoy yummy food
- Have fun playing
- Make memories with family and friends
