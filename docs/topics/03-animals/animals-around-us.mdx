## Animals Have Different Bodies

### Some animals have:
- **Fur** (like dogs and cats)
- **Feathers** (like birds)
- **Scales** (like fish and snakes)
- **Shells** (like turtles and snails)
- **Skin** (like frogs and elephants)

### Some animals have:
- **4 legs** (like dogs, cats, horses)
- **2 legs** (like chickens and humans)
- **No legs** (like snakes and fish)
- **Many legs** (like spiders and centipedes)

## How Animals Move

Different animals move in different ways:
- **Walk or run** - dogs, horses, elephants
- **Fly** - birds, butterflies, bats
- **Swim** - fish, dolphins, ducks
- **Hop** - frogs, kangaroos, rabbits
- **Slither** - snakes, worms
- **Crawl** - babies, caterpillars
