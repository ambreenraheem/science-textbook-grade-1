# Animals

Welcome to the wonderful world of animals! Get ready to learn about creatures big and small!

## What Will You Learn?

In this topic, you will discover:

- Different kinds of animals around us
- Where animals live (their homes)
- What animals eat
- Sounds that animals make

## Lessons

1. **Animals Around Us** - Meet different types of animals
2. **Animal Homes** - Learn where animals live
3. **What Animals Eat** - Discover what animals like to eat
4. **Animal Sounds** - Listen to sounds animals make

## Why Is This Important?

Learning about animals helps you:
- Understand the animal world
- Take care of pets and wildlife
- Appreciate nature
- Stay safe around animals

Let's begin our animal adventure!
