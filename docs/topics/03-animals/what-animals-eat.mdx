## Animal Teeth and Food

Animals have different teeth for different foods!

### Flat Teeth
- Cows have flat teeth
- Good for grinding plants
- Chew side to side

### Sharp Teeth
- Lions have sharp teeth
- Good for eating meat
- Can bite hard!

### Both Kinds
- Dogs have flat AND sharp teeth
- Can eat plants and meat
- Just like you!

## What You Learned Today

- Some animals eat only plants (herbivores): cows, rabbits, elephants
- Some animals eat only meat (carnivores): lions, tigers, sharks
- Some animals eat both (omnivores): bears, dogs, pigs
- Animals have different teeth for different foods
- Animals get food by hunting, grazing, or foraging
