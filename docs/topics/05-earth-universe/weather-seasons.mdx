# Weather and Seasons

Every day the weather is a little different! And every year we have four special seasons! Let's learn about them!

## What is Weather?

Weather is what it's like outside right now!

### Questions About Weather
- Is it sunny or cloudy?
- Is it hot or cold?
- Is it rainy or dry?
- Is it windy or calm?

## Why Do We Have Seasons?

### Earth Tilts!
- Earth is tilted like a leaning top
- As Earth goes around the sun...
- Different parts get more sunlight
- More sunlight = warmer (summer)
- Less sunlight = cooler (winter)
