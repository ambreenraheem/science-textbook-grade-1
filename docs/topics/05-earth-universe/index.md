# Earth and Universe

Welcome to our biggest adventure yet! Let's explore our amazing planet Earth and the incredible universe beyond!

## What Will You Learn?

In this topic, you will discover:

- Why we have day and night
- All about the sun, moon, and stars
- Different types of weather and seasons
- How special our planet Earth is

## Lessons

1. **Day and Night** - Learn why the sky changes from bright to dark
2. **Sun, Moon, and Stars** - Meet the objects in our sky
3. **Weather and Seasons** - Discover rain, sun, snow, and the four seasons
4. **Our Planet Earth** - Explore our beautiful home

## Why Is This Important?

Learning about Earth and space helps you:
- Understand why we have day and night
- Know what makes weather change
- Appreciate how special our planet is
- Dream about the stars and beyond!

Let's blast off on our space adventure!
