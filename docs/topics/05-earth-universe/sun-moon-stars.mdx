# Sun, Moon, and Stars

Look up at the sky! There are amazing things to see! Let's learn about the sun, moon, and stars!

The sun is our special star!

### What is the Sun?
- A giant ball of fire
- Very, very hot
- Gives us light
- Gives us warmth

### Why the Sun is Important
- Makes daytime bright
- Helps plants grow
- Keeps us warm
- Gives us energy

### Fun Sun Facts
- The sun is HUGE! A million Earths could fit inside!
- It's very far away
- Never look directly at the sun - it can hurt your eyes!
- The sun is a star, just like the ones we see at night!

### The Sun Helps Us
- Dries our clothes on the line
- Makes the beach warm
- Helps flowers bloom
- Gives us vitamin D!

### Sun Safety âš ï¸
- Never stare at the sun
- Wear sunglasses outside
- Use sunscreen
- Wear a hat on sunny days

### What is the Moon?
- A big round rock
- Goes around Earth
- Shines at night
- Much smaller than the sun

### Where Does Moon Light Come From?
- The moon doesn't make its own light!
- It reflects light from the sun
- Like a mirror in the sky!
- That's why it glows at night!

## The Stars â­

Stars are like sparkles in the night sky!

### What are Stars?
- Balls of hot gas
- Just like our sun!
- Very far away
- That's why they look tiny

### Why Stars Twinkle
- Light travels very far to reach us
- Air makes the light wobble
- That's why they seem to twinkle!
- Like a candle flickering!

### So Many Stars!
- There are billions and billions of stars!
- More stars than grains of sand on a beach!
- We can only see some of them
- They're always there, even during the day!

### Star Pictures (Constellations)
