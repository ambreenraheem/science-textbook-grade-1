## Why Do We Have Day and Night?

Here's the amazing secret!

### Earth Spins! ğŸŒ
- Our planet Earth is like a big ball
- It spins around and around
- One spin takes 24 hours
- That's one whole day!

### How It Works
1. When your side faces the sun = DAY
2. Earth keeps spinning
3. Your side turns away from the sun = NIGHT
4. Earth keeps spinning
5. Your side faces the sun again = DAY

### Like a Spinning Top!
Imagine you are standing on a spinning top:
- Sometimes you face a lamp (that's day!)
- Sometimes you face away from the lamp (that's night!)
- The top keeps spinning, so it keeps changing!

## The Sun Never Stops Shining!

Here's something cool to know:
- The sun always shines
- It never turns off
- When it's night for us...
- It's day for people on the other side of Earth!

### Day and Night Around the World
- When you sleep, kids far away are playing!
- When you eat breakfast, others eat dinner!
- Earth is big and always spinning!

## Shadows During the Day

The sun makes shadows!

### Morning Shadows
- Sun is low in the sky
- Shadows are long
- They point one way

### Noon Shadows
- Sun is high up
- Shadows are short
- Almost under you!

### Evening Shadows
- Sun is low again
- Shadows are long again
- They point the other way!

## What You Learned Today

- Day is when we face the sun; night is when we face away
- Earth spins around like a top, taking 24 hours
- Morning starts with sunrise; evening ends with sunset
- The sun is always shining - just not always on our side!
- While we sleep, people on the other side of Earth are awake!
