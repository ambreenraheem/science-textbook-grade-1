# What Plants Need to Grow

Just like you need food and water, plants need special things to grow big and strong! Let's find out what they are!

## Four Things Plants Need

### 1. Sunlight â˜€ï¸

Plants LOVE the sun!

**Why plants need sunlight:**
- Leaves use sunlight to make food
- Without sun, plants cannot eat
- Plants grow toward the light
- Different plants need different amounts

**Try this:** Watch how a plant in your window leans toward the sun!

**What happens without sunlight?**
- Leaves turn yellow
- Plant becomes weak
- It stops growing
- It may die

### 2. Water ğŸ’§

Plants get thirsty too!

**Why plants need water:**
- Roots drink water from the soil
- Water helps move food inside the plant
- Keeps leaves fresh and green
- Too much or too little is bad!

**How do we water plants?**
- Use a watering can
- Pour water on the soil, not the leaves
- Water when the soil feels dry
- Don't give too much water!

**What happens without water?**
- Leaves droop and wilt
- Plant becomes dry
- Soil cracks
- Plant cannot make food

### 3. Air ğŸ’¨

Yes, plants breathe!

**Why plants need air:**
- Leaves take in air through tiny holes
- Air has things plants need
- Plants also give us fresh air back!
- This happens all day long

**Fun fact:** Plants breathe in what we breathe out, and breathe out what we breathe in! We help each other!

**What happens without air?**
- Plants cannot make food
- They cannot grow
- They become sick

### 4. Soil ğŸª´

Soil is plant food's home!

**Why plants need soil:**
- Roots grow in soil
- Soil holds water for roots
- Soil has yummy nutrients
- Keeps the plant standing up

**What is in soil?**
- Tiny pieces of rock
- Dead leaves
- Water
- Air pockets
- Little bugs that help!

**What happens without good soil?**
- Roots cannot grow well
- Plant cannot stand up
- Not enough nutrients to eat

## Taking Care of Plants

You can help plants grow!

### Do's âœ…
- Give water when soil is dry
- Put plants near windows for sun
- Use good soil
- Talk to your plants (they like it!)
- Remove dead leaves

### Don'ts âŒ
- Too much water (roots can rot!)
- No sunlight (plant gets sick)
- Forgetting to water
- Pulling on leaves
- Keeping in very cold places

## What You Learned Today

- Plants need four things: sunlight, water, air, and soil
- Sunlight helps leaves make food
- Water keeps plants healthy and fresh
- Air helps plants breathe
- Soil gives plants nutrients and holds them up
- Too much or too little of anything is bad for plants!
