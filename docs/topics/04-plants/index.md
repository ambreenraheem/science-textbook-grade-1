# Plants

Welcome to the amazing world of plants! Let's learn about the green living things all around us!

## What Will You Learn?

In this topic, you will discover:

- The different parts of a plant
- What plants need to grow
- Different types of plants
- How plants help us every day

## Lessons

1. **Parts of a Plant** - Learn about roots, stems, leaves, and flowers
2. **What Plants Need** - Discover what helps plants grow big and strong
3. **Types of Plants** - Meet trees, flowers, vegetables, and more
4. **How Plants Help Us** - See all the ways plants make our lives better

## Why Is This Important?

Learning about plants helps you:
- Understand how living things grow
- Take care of your own plants
- Know where your food comes from
- Appreciate nature and the environment

Let's grow our knowledge about plants!
